Pre-Demo Setup & Preparation

Technical Setup Checklist (15 minutes before demo)

  • Test internet connection and backup options
  • Launch Seiketsu AI demo environment
  • Prepare test phone numbers for live calls
  • Load prospect's CRM data into demo system
  • Set up screen sharing with high-quality audio
  • Have backup video recordings ready
  • Test all integrations (CRM, calendar, etc.)

Prospect Research & Customization

Research Points (30 minutes before demo):

  • Company size and current lead volume
  • Existing CRM and tech stack
  • Current lead response processes
  • Pain points mentioned in discovery calls
  • Competitive landscape and alternatives considered
  • Decision-making process and timeline
